Gluten-Free pizza recipe

History:

In 2017 my sister was diagnosed with Celiac, a genetic disease which made her body unable to process wheat. Pizza, up to this point, had been pretty staple in my family. It was easy to prepare and everyone liked it. My sister, however, only really liked the crusts of the pizza and since she had celiac she could no longer eat it. A couple years later my dad decided he was fed up with all the Gluten-Free pizzas and decided to make his own. You see, making good dough without wheat is really hard so most of the Gluten-Free pizza dough on the market was just not good, so it took a couple of attempts to make good (Some of these failed attempts were borderline disgusting) However my dad eventually found some good dough, refined the recipe, and he created some genuinely amazing pizza. For reference, that dough is the Bob's Red Mill Pizza Dough

Ingredients:

Before making this pizza you will need the following materials

  • Gluten Free Dough (Bob's Red Mill!)
  • Yeast
  • Eggs
  • Tablespoon of olive oil
  • Toppings
    • Pepperoni
    • Mozzarella
    • Tomato Sauce
    • Onion
    • Basil
    • What ever else you want
  • Cup and a half of hot water

Instructions:

  1. Mix all ingredients up (Except the Toppings)
  2. Let it sit roughly 20-30 minutes
  3. Flatten out the dough (Use serane wrap, Gluten Free dough is stickier than normal dough)
  4. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees fagrenheit
  5. Put the pizaa on a tray (Use parchment paper, dough is sticky!)
  6. Put the pizza on the tray into the oven
  7. Put the pizza on the tray into the oven
  8. Cook for 7 minutes
    • Whilst the pizza is cooking prepare your toppings (e.g Slice Mozzarella)
  9. Take the dough out after 7 minutes and the toppings on in the following order
    1. Tomato Sauce
    2. Mozzarella
    3. all other Toppings
  10. Put back in the oven for ~15 minutes
  11. Enjoy!
